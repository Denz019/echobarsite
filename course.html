Cocktail Course

Join us for a fun and interactive cocktail course where you’ll learn the basics of bartending while tasting delicious cocktails along the way.

After a short introduction to cocktail theory, you’ll step behind the bar to shake, stir, and mix your own drinks.

The course is perfect for bachelor(ette) parties, team-building events, or anyone looking for a unique start to the evening.

Every course is customized to suit the group, with the option to add a little friendly competition for extra fun.

Booking Information

Our cocktail courses are tailored to each party, which means both the content and pricing are adjusted to your group’s wishes and size.

A minimum of four people is required to book a course.

Whether you are planning a private celebration or a company event, we will create an experience that fits your occasion perfectly.

For bookings and questions, please contact us at hi@echobar.dk.

Wanna add a Private Silent Disco?

For the ultimate party starter, you can combine your cocktail course with one hour of private silent disco.

Choose your own songs and playlists, put on the headphones, and keep the party going in a fun and unique way.

The silent disco is a perfect addition for groups who want to continue the energy after mixing cocktails behind the bar.

Just let us know when booking if you would like to add the silent disco experience to your event.
