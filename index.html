ABOUT US

At Echo Bar, it’s never just about the drinks — it’s about the experience. We’ve created a space in the heart of Copenhagen where handcrafted cocktails, great music, and an unforgettable atmosphere come together in one unique nightlife destination. From the first drink at the bar to the final song of the night, Echo is a place where people connect, unwind, and enjoy moments that feel effortless and memorable.

Echo Bar blends the intimacy of a cocktail lounge with the energy of a silent disco. We believe the best nights are created through atmosphere, attention to detail, and people who feel welcome the moment they walk through the door. Whether you’re stopping by for after-work cocktails, date night, or a full night out with friends, our goal is simple — to create experiences that stay with you long after the night ends.
